## Why One Slice Is Enough For This Pilot

The pilot is not trying to ship Quasar Vault Guard. It is trying to prove the
factory can enforce a high-friction card without weak evidence or authority
leakage. A larger graph would add volume, not stronger proof.

## Completion Contract

The slice can be completed only when:

- worker packets exist;
- visual evidence exists;
- security evidence exists;
- Auditor preflight exists;
- independent review exists;
- human gate record exists;
- Receipt Five exists;
